guide

How to test your phone speaker

Follow these simple steps to run a complete speaker test and understand each result.

1

Adjust volume

Set your phone volume to about 80% (or comfortable level). Use the slider to adjust test volume.

2

Play a test

Tap any test button — Left, Right, Stereo, or Sweep. Listen carefully to the audio through your speaker.

3

Check clarity

Listen for distortion, crackling, or muffled sound at different frequencies (low, mid, high).

4

Test stereo

Use Left and Right tests to check if both channels work. Stereo test plays alternating channels.

📱 iPhone speaker test

Works on all iPhones via Safari. Tests both earpiece and main speakers.

🤖 Android speaker test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Tests mono and stereo speakers.

🔊 Test in a quiet room

Background noise can mask distortion. Test in a quiet environment for accurate results.

🎧 Try headphones too

Plug in headphones to test your phone's headphone jack or Bluetooth audio output.
