guide

How to test your phone camera

Follow these simple steps to run a complete camera phone test and understand each result.

1

Grant camera access

Tap "Start Camera" and allow your browser to use the camera. This is required for the test.

2

Check focus

Point your camera at an object with text or fine detail. Tap to focus — sharpness = pass.

3

Test flash & zoom

Toggle the flash and use pinch-to-zoom. The test will confirm if both work as expected.

4

Review results

Each check shows a pass or fail. Use the snapshot to inspect image quality visually.

📱 iPhone camera tester

Works on iPhone 6 through iPhone 15 and newer via Safari. Tests both front and rear cameras.

🤖 Android camera test

Compatible with Chrome, Edge, and Samsung Internet on Android devices. Tests all available cameras.

💡 Best lighting

Test in a well-lit environment for the most accurate focus and color results. Avoid backlight.

🧽 Clean your lens

A smudged lens can affect sharpness. Wipe it gently before running the test.
