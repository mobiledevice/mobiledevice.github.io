guide

How to test your phone Bluetooth

Follow these simple steps to run a complete Bluetooth test and understand each result.

1

Start the scan

Tap "Start Scan" and allow Bluetooth permissions if prompted. The test will begin scanning.

2

Wait for devices

Your phone will search for nearby Bluetooth devices. This may take 10-30 seconds.

3

Check results

Devices found will appear in the list with their names and signal strength.

4

Verify functionality

If devices are found, your Bluetooth radio is working. If none appear, try moving closer to a Bluetooth device.

📱 iPhone Bluetooth test

Works on iPhones with iOS 13+ via Safari. Uses Web Bluetooth API for device discovery.

🤖 Android Bluetooth test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Tests Bluetooth discovery on Android devices.

📶 Check signal strength

Stronger signals (closer to 0 dBm) indicate better Bluetooth reception and closer devices.

🔋 Turn on Bluetooth

Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone before starting the scan for best results.
