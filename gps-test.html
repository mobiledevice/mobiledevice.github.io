How to test your GPS accuracy

Follow these simple steps to run a complete GPS and location test and understand each result.

1

Start the test

Tap "Start GPS Test" and allow location access when prompted. The test will begin reading GPS data.

2

Wait for fix

Your device needs a few seconds to get a GPS lock. The map will show your position when locked.

3

Check accuracy

Look at the accuracy reading in meters. Lower numbers mean more accurate GPS positioning.

4

Move around

Walk a few steps and watch the position update. This confirms your GPS is tracking movement.

📱 iPhone GPS test

Works on all iPhones via Safari. Uses the device's built-in GPS chip for accurate positioning.

🤖 Android GPS test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Tests GPS accuracy with real-time positioning.

🌤️ Go outside

GPS works best outdoors with a clear view of the sky. Indoor positioning may be less accurate.
