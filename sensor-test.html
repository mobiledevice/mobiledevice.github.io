guide

How to test your phone motion sensors

Follow these simple steps to run a complete accelerometer and gyroscope test and understand each result.

1

Start the test

Tap "Start sensors" and grant motion permission if prompted. The 3D cube will appear.

2

Tilt your phone

Tilt your phone forward/backward and side to side. Watch the cube respond to your movements.

3

Rotate your phone

Rotate your phone in different directions. The gyroscope data should update smoothly.

4

Check the values

Look at the X, Y, Z values. They should change smoothly as you move your phone.

📱 iPhone sensor test

Works on all iPhones via Safari. Tests both accelerometer and gyroscope sensors.

🤖 Android sensor test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Tests all motion sensors on Android devices.

🌀 Move smoothly

Move your phone in smooth motions for the most accurate sensor response. Jerky movements can cause data spikes.

📊 Check all axes

Test all three axes (X, Y, Z) by moving your phone in different directions to ensure all sensors work.

An accelerometer measures linear acceleration and tilt (gravity). A gyroscope measures angular velocity and rotation. Together, they provide full motion tracking for apps like games, navigation, and AR.
