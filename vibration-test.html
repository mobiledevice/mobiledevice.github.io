guide

How to test your phone vibration motor

Follow these simple steps to run a complete vibration test and understand each result.

1

Hold your phone

Hold your phone in your hand so you can feel the vibration clearly when it triggers.

2

Test a pattern

Tap the "Vibrate" button or any pattern button. Your phone should vibrate immediately.

3

Try different patterns

Test short, long, double, and custom patterns to check if the motor responds correctly.

4

Check the results

Note if the vibration feels consistent and strong. Listen for any unusual noises.

📱 iPhone vibration test

Works on all iPhones via Safari. Tests both the Taptic Engine and traditional vibration motors.

🤖 Android vibration test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Tests vibration motors on all Android devices.

👂 Listen carefully

A grinding or rattling sound may indicate a damaged vibration motor or loose component.

📊 Test multiple patterns

Different patterns help identify if the motor responds correctly to varying durations and intensities.
