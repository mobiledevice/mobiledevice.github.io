our mission

Zero-friction hardware diagnostics

We believe testing your phone shouldn't require bloating your storage with heavy apps or paying bench fees at a repair shop shop.

Mobile Device Testing Tools was born out of a simple frustration: trying to verify if a device works reliably before buying or selling it without compromising personal privacy. Most modern testing suites demand unnecessary app permissions, serve intrusive pop-up trackers, or lock basic sensor readouts behind paywalls.

Our toolkit relies entirely on high-performance native Web APIs. When you trigger a camera grid, microphone sampler, or gyroscope coordinate matrix, our scripts hook directly into your browser sandbox, compile the hardware response locally, and output a strict binary Pass/Fail result instantly. Nothing leaves your phone.
