How to check your phone battery health

Follow these simple steps to run a complete battery health check and understand each result.

1

Open the test

Tap "Check Battery" to start the test. The tool will read your device's battery data.

2

View battery level

Check your current battery percentage. The battery icon fills up to show your charge level.

3

Check charging status

See if your phone is currently charging. The status will show "Charging" or "Not charging".

4

Review health score

The health score estimates your battery's condition. Higher scores mean better battery health.

📱 iPhone battery health

Works on all iPhones via Safari. Provides battery level and estimated health based on available data.

🤖 Android battery test

Compatible with Chrome and Samsung Internet. Reads battery data using the Battery Status API.

🔋 Check multiple times

Battery health estimates improve with multiple readings. Check at different charge levels for accuracy.

⚡ Test while charging

Testing while charging can help identify if your battery holds charge properly.
