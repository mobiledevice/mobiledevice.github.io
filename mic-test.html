How to test your phone microphone

Follow these simple steps to run a complete mic test and understand each result.

1

Grant mic access

Tap "Start mic test" and allow your browser to use the microphone. This is required for the test.

2

Speak into your phone

Talk normally, whisper, or test different volumes. The visualizer shows your sound levels in real time.

3

Check the results

Review your volume levels, peak dB, and average. The test will tell you if your mic is working properly.

4

Test playback (optional)

Use the playback button to hear your recorded audio and check for clarity and distortion.
